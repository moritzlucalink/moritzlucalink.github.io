hi :)
from january 2021 to december 2024 i was a doctoral student in the working group numerical optimization at the university of konstanz in germany. my position was funded by the transferplatform at university of konstanz which aimed to include notions of homeowner acceptance of retrofit measures into multi-objective energy supply network models.

consequently, my research focused on both, modeling energy supply networks as well as developing algorithms able to solve the resulting multi-objective mixed-integer nonlinear optimization problems.

currently, i am waiting for the defence of my doctoral thesis which will take place around april 2025. in the meantime, i work on the revisions of two academic papers as well as on the development of a bot intended to support in-game sports betting on the basis of reinforcement learning.
