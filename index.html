hi :)
i am phd student in the working group numerical optimization at the university of konstanz in germany. my position is funded by the bmbf project elan which aims to develop efficient model predictive control methods for the local utilization of waste heat in low-temperature heating networks.

my research focuses on non-linear and non-smooth pde-constrained optimization, optimal control, reinforcement learning and model reduction of large-scale dynamical systems.

additionally, i am a member of the young mathematicians in model order reduction (ymmor) committee.
