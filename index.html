hi :)
i am a mathematician specializing in algorithm development and transforming real-world challenges into mathematical models. i love tackling complex problems that drive meaningful impact and contribute to a sustainable, thriving future. to make this happen, i believe that data-driven methods and mathematical optimization algorithms can be incredibly beneficial - when applied wisely and in the right context.

from january 2021 to december 2024, i was a doctoral student in the numerical optimization research group at the university of konstanz in germany. my position was funded by the transferplatform, an initiave at the university of konstanz focused on integrating homeowner acceptance of retrofit measures into multi-objective energy supply network models.

through my research, i have developed expertise in identifying key questions within real-world challenges by thoroughly exploring the respective domain, engaging with domain experts, and translating these insights into mathematical models. i apply suitable optimization techniques - or develop new ones when necessary - to effectively address these problems. when designing new algorithms, i am used to ensure a full theoretical and computational analysis to guarantee their correctness and efficiency. i am always eager to explore new methods and stay up to date with the latest developments in optimization and applied mathematics. my curiosity drives me to continuously expand my knowledge and refine my approaches, ensuring i can tackle emerging challenges with cutting-edge techniques.
i have a strong experience in coding with python, particularly in developing and integrating optimization software packages such as pyomo, gurobi, and scip. as a strong supporter of open-source, i actively share my work on my github page, making my codes accessible to everybody.

the defence of my doctoral thesis is expected to take place around april 2025. in the meantime, i work on the revisions of two academic papers as well as on the development of a bot intended to support in-game sports betting on the basis of reinforcement learning.
